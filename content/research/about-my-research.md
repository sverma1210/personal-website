## Artifacts

#### CodeSSM
- The code for our CodeSSM model is available at [https://github.com/abx04/CodeSSM](https://github.com/abx04/CodeSSM) and the weights for 1B base models are available at [https://huggingface.co/abx15462/codessm-1b](https://huggingface.co/abx15462/codessm-1b).
