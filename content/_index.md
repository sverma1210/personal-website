I am a Doctoral Researcher at [Software Technology Group](https://www.stg.tu-darmstadt.de/research_stg/index.en.jsp), TU Darmstadt supervised by [Mira Mezini](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mira_Mezini). I am currently working on State Space Models and Diffusion models. My research interest lies in efficient and interpretable models. I developed efficient SSM-based models for code understanding, SSM-based diffusion models for code understanding and generation, and interpretability framework for SSM kernel analysis. I have previously worked at Rakuten Mobile, Tokyo (JP) on automation and efficient handover in 5G ORAN. I have worked on multi-agent systems and Reinforcement Learning for user equipment power optimization during handovers in 5G. I also developed 3GPP compliant network simulator for 5G.
